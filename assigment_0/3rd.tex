Task 1 \\
let $ L_{n}(\hat{y}, y) = \frac{1}{N}(\sum_{i=1}^{N}{|\hat{y}_i - y_i|^n})$$
\\
$ MSE = L_{2}(\hat{y}, y)$$
\\
$ MAE = L_{1}(\hat{y}, y) $$
